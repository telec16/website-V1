Generative music

Create randomly generated music with markov chains!

  1. Choose the sound (WIP)
  2. Choose the tempo, the note duration, and the after-note blank duration
  3. Add notes (A-G, optional #/b, and +/- to change octave)
  4. Change the probabilities in the matrix
  5. Enjoy!
